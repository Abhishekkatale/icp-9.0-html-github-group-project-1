    Entrepreneurship Development Cell

    Started as Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Technology, Pimpri, Pune in May, 2012 under AICTE grant for the development of Entrepreneurship ecosystem. Since then, the cell is creating the entrepreneurial ecosystem by building strong & confident minds interested in this domain. EDSI Cell of DIT is committed to inspire, motivate, guide and support every interested and passionate, who aspires to grow and create oneself as an Innovator or Entrepreneur. EDSI Cell provides ample opportunities to expose & explore the entrepreneurial environment and help to bear the fruits out of just a bare seed. Since its beginning in 2012, the Cell has developed a rich and diverse set of offerings that cater first to the needs of the students, alumni, faculty and society in line with the Vision and Mission of the Institute. In this journey coming a long, it is proud to share that our alumni are established as entrepreneurs in various fields of their interest worldwide. Few of the recent noteworthy laurels are - · 5 Star Rating by Ministry of Education for the work done by the Institute’s Startup & Innovation Cell. · Starts ups incubated by Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) – Pinnacle Industry, Pune. · Association with ecosystem enablers. These accolades have spread a word creating a wave of entrepreneurial mindset among the aspirants on the campus
